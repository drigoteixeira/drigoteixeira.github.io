Hi! I'm Rodrigo. I live in São Paulo, Brazil, currently working at Porto Seguro as UX Designer.

I consider myself a designer who digs into business problems, analyses, and solves them with the help of the creative process.

To accomplish this, I focus on delivering key outcomes by diagnosing problems, scaling, and creating optimal solutions that matter for people and businesses.

During my career, I've been working with small and large companies, such as Itaú Unibanco, AVON, Riachuelo, Atlantic Energias Renováveis, Discovery Turbo, DesenvolveSP, CET, and Sao Paulo State Government.

Additionally, I believe in collaboration. I am regularly in contact with other experts and specialists in a way to get distinctive points of view to improve the final product or service. This is an opportunity to learn more and get better as a designer.
