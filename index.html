About me

I am a highly experienced and versatile digital product designer with a strategic mindset and over 15 years of experience in the conception and execution of digital products and services.

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of working with a diverse range of companies, from startups to industry giants, including Linx, Vale, Porto Seguro, Itaú Unibanco, AVON, Riachuelo, Atlantic Renewable Energies, Discovery Turbo, DesenvolveSP, and the São Paulo State Government.

I'm committed to translating business strategies into user-centered design solutions through extensive research, journey mapping, opportunity identification, interface development, and prototyping.

In recent years, I have expanded my role to encompass the operation and guidance of design teams. I am proficient in managing design tools and processes, fostering career growth within UX teams, and effectively leading design projects.
