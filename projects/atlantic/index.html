New winds move the future in Brazil

A new fresh design for a new company

Client
Atlantic Energias Renováveis
Year
2016
Services
Strategy, visual and digital marketing (SEO)

Goal

Atlantic Energias Renováveis is a holding company that develops, owns and operates renewable energy projects

I was tasked to redesign their existing website, using flexible elements to improve their digital presence. The main goal was to create a simple and sleek website to represent their position in the market.

Homepage

The main challenge was to use a video on the homepage, highlighting some shots from some company's wind farms. We chose to use a short video, making some improvements with the front-end team.

Another decision was to build simple navigation that will expand in the future. Additionally, we rethought the navigation in the mobile, using some best practices to tap in links, menus using a good typography size.

Results

With a new website, Atlantic won a great position in the renewable energy market in Brazil. In a month, the website achieved around 694.928 views with an engagement of 3 pages per visit.

In 2019, the Atlantic was acquired by China General Nuclear Power Group (CGN).
