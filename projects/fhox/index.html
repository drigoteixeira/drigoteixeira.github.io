FHOX is one of the most known and admired companies in the Brazil's photography segment. As part of its digital presence strategy, the objective was to redesign their main portal and improve their brand's unique values.

The overall interface is minimal in order to stablish a clean focus on content. The navigation is full screen which helps the user easily immerse in the experience.

Thumbs, galleries and news lists were designed to highlight the main FHOX subject: The photography.
