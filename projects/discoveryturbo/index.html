Discovery Turbo was launched on the Brazillian PayTV in 2014. We at A2ad were tasked to design a hot site to present the new channel and also to give the brand a modern and friendly feel to show the love to motors and velocity.

Role

During the project, my responsibilities included organizing meetings with the Content Strategists, Design and Development team and also, designing the information architecture (sitemaps), wireframing and the UI Design.

Highlights

The hotsite needed to be hosted in a different place and connected by an iframe with the Discovery servers, which required serious efforts to build a friendly SEO and also a good responsive website.

As part of the project, we chose to build the hotsite entirely in WordPress that gives content editors the ability to add/edit all of the content on the site easily and gives us control to make adjustments on the responsive version.

Even temporary, the project had a great acceptance by the public. The hotsite had 12% of engagement and 31 million of people were impacted.
