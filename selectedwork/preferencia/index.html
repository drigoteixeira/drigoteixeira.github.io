Preferência a Vida.

Digital Campaign Compilation / A2 Comunicação, 2011

Preferência a Vida was a internet/social campaign about the importance of crosswalk in the City of São Paulo. We was tasked to create a blog that highlighted "The Crosswalk Man". The project was awarded by Effie Awards Brazil, in 2012.

Project Roles
Information Architecture, UI Design
