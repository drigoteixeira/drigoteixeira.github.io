Minha Claro.

A2ad - 2014 - Pitch / UX and Visual Design.

Created by Claro, one of the most important mobile companies of the Latin America, Minha Claro is a service through which the clients can get information and solve issues about your mobile and TV plans. In this concept, we tried to delivery a fully responsive design, using nice call to actions and providing information to engage the user across the site.
