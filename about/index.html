Hello!

Hi! I'm Rodrigo. I live in São Paulo, Brazil, currently working at EY Design Studio LAS, focused in UX Design and Strategy.

During my career, I have been working with small and large companies, such as Linx, Porto Seguro, Itaú Unibanco, AVON, Riachuelo, Atlantic Energias Renováveis, Discovery Turbo, DesenvolveSP, Dersa, CET, and Sao Paulo State Government.

Additionally, I believe in collaboration. I am regularly in contact with other experts and specialists in a way to get distinctive points of view to improve the outcome. This is an opportunity to learn more and get better as a designer.
