About

Design is how I think through complex problems.

Hi, I'm Rodrigo. I live in São Paulo, Brazil. I'm an UX Specialist focused in UX Strategy & Service Design.

During my career, I have been working with small and large companies, such as Linx, Stone.co, Vale S.A, Porto Seguro, Itaú Unibanco, AVON, Riachuelo, Atlantic Energias Renováveis, Discovery Turbo, DesenvolveSP, Dersa, CET, and Sao Paulo State Government.

Additionally, I believe in collaboration. I am regularly in contact with other experts and specialists in a way to get distinctive points of view to improve the outcome. This is an opportunity to learn more and get better as a designer.

Approach

I'm committed to translating business strategies into user-centered design solutions through extensive research, journey mapping, opportunity identification, interface development, and prototyping.

In recent years, I have expanded my role to encompass the operation and guidance of design teams. I am proficient in managing design tools and processes, fostering career growth within UX teams, and effectively leading design projects.
