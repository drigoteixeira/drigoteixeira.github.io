About me

I'm Rodrigo Teixeira, Digital Designer living in São Paulo. Currently working at A2ad as Lead Designer.

I have over ten years of hands-on experience in designing digital products and services, ensuring high-quality and exercising diplomatic client and team management skills.

Although I consider myself a designer foremost, heading UX discipline, I do a bit of everything that goes into a digital product, from defining product goals and features to copy-writing to front-end development.
