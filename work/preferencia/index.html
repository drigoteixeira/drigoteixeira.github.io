Preferência à Vida.

A2 Comunicação - 2011 - Pitch / Information Architecture and Visual Design.

Preferência a Vida was a internet/social campaign about the importance of crosswalk in the City of São Paulo. We was tasked to create a blog that introduced "The Crosswalk Man". The project was awarded by Effie Awards Brazil, in 2012.
